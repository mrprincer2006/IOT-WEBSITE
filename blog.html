Saving Energy with IoT

Saving energy with the Internet of Things (IoT) involves using smart sensors and devices to collect real-time data on energy consumption and then automatically adjust systems like lighting, heating, and cooling. This optimizes usage, reduces waste, and lowers costs — delivering financial and environmental benefits for homes, buildings, and smart cities. Key applications include smart thermostats, smart lighting, appliances, and data-driven energy management for smart grids.
